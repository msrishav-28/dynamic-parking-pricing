Dynamic Parking Pricing Model

Project Overview

This project develops and analyzes dynamic pricing models for parking lots, leveraging historical occupancy, traffic, and special event data. The objective is to optimize parking prices to maximize revenue while managing demand effectively through three distinct pricing models.

Key Findings & Recommendations

Strategic Recommendations

  • Parameter Optimization: Fine-tune model coefficients using historical revenue data and A/B testing
  • Real-time Integration: Implement live data feeds for occupancy, traffic, and competitor pricing
  • Predictive Analytics: Incorporate forecasting to enable proactive price adjustments
  • User Experience: Develop clear pricing communication strategies to maintain customer satisfaction
  • Robustness Testing: Validate models under various demand scenarios and competitive strategies

The Competitive Pricing Model (Model 3) demonstrates the most sophisticated approach by balancing internal demand factors with external market conditions, making it the recommended solution for implementation.
